Orthopedician

A medical specialty that involves the diagnosis, treatment, correction, and prevention of skeletal deformities in patients is known as orthopaedics. The deformities may be disorders of elements that make up the musculoskeletal system such as muscles, bones, ligaments, joints, nerves, tendons, and skin. Orthopaedists or orthopaedic surgeons are doctors who specialise in the field of orthopaedics.
